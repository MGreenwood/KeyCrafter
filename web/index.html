About KeyCrafter

KeyCrafter is a typing-based crafting game where you type words to gather resources and craft items. It's a fun and challenging way to improve your typing skills while exploring a world of crafting and building.

Type & Craft

Improve your typing skills while gathering resources and crafting tools.

Resource Gathering

Collect wood, copper, and more by typing words to move and harvest.

️ Crafting System

Build workbenches, upgrade tools, and enhance your gathering abilities.

Progress Tracking

Track your typing speed, accuracy, as well as your game progress automatically!

Installation

Windows

irm play.keycrafter.fun/install.ps1 | iex

Linux/Mac

curl -fsSL play.keycrafter.fun/install.sh | bash

Getting Started

After installation, you can start playing:

keycrafter

To keep your game up to date:

keycrafter update
